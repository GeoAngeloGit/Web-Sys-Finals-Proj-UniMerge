Four Steps to Success

Step 1: Authenticate

Enter your Gmail address and 16-character App Password. We verify the connection immediately so you know your setup is perfect before uploading any data.

Step 2: Upload Assets

Upload your CSV or Excel list. If sending certificates, upload your ZIP folder. Our system handles the extraction and mapping logic automatically.

Step 3: Map and Sompose

Choose which columns contain the email addresses and filenames. Design your email using {{Tags}} to insert recipient data dynamically.

Step 4: Send and Monitor

Click Start Bulk Send to enter the Live Dashboard. Once complete, download any needed failure reports and click Done to clear your session.
